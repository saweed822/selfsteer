# ✅ MASTER ROADMAP — Visit Kashmir Tours & Travels Website

---

## 1. 🎯 Core Business Goal

Build a **modern, premium-looking tourism website** for:

**Brand Name:** Visit Kashmir Tours & Travels
**Primary Services:**

1. Tour & Travel Packages
2. Car Rental Services

**Primary UX Requirement:**
➡ Immediately show 2 clear choices:

* Book Tour Packages
* Book Car Rentals

NO forms, NO input boxes, NO booking systems.
ONLY contact popup on booking click.

---

## 2. 🎨 Design Philosophy (Very Important)

### UI Style

* Modern
* Premium tourism feel
* Clean spacing
* Large visuals
* Glassmorphism or soft shadow cards
* Smooth micro animations
* Mobile-first responsive

### Color Direction (Suggested)

* Primary: Deep Kashmir Blue / Mountain Teal
* Accent: Saffron / Warm Gold
* Background: White or very light gradient

### Font Pairing

* Headings: Poppins / Montserrat
* Body: Inter / Open Sans

```
Home

 ├ Contact Popup System (Global Component)
```
